Welcome to Dean Guitars!

Dean Guitars was founded in Chicago, Illinois, in 1976 by Dean Zelinsky, but came to prominence under Elliott Rubinson in 1997 after his company, Armadillo Enterprises, purchased the Dean trade name. Dean guitars and basses have been seen in such bands as Heart, Kansas, Pantera, Michael Schenker Group, Megadeth, The Cars, Molly Hachet, Triumph, and ZZ Top
